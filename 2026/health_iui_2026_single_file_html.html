About the Workshop

As AI reshapes health and care, intelligent user interfaces (IUIs) must be safe, transparent, and engaging for patients, clinicians, and caregivers. HealthIUI convenes HCI, AI, and health communities to share methods, systems, and evaluations that improve long-term engagement, personalization, and trust.

Co-located with ACM IUI 2026 — March 23–26, 2026 • Paphos, Cyprus.

Call for Papers

We invite full papers (max 16 pages excl. refs) and short papers (max 8 pages excl. refs), one-column ACM format. We welcome research, system, dataset, position, and replication papers. All submissions are peer-reviewed and will follow ACM IUI workshop publication standards.

  • English, PDF, one-column ACM template (≥9pt)
  • Include author names, affiliations, and contact emails
  • Optional: links to demos, data, or code
  • Original work; not under review elsewhere

Topics of Interest

Personalized Health Interventions

Modeling, adaptation, and evaluation for long-term adherence.

Human-AI Collaboration

Decision support, copilots, and safety in clinical workflows.

Ethics, Privacy, & Trust

Bias, explainability, consent, and governance in health IUIs.

Wearables & Sensing

Edge/ambient sensing, data quality, feedback design.

Behavioral & Mental Health

Support, safety planning, and equitable access.

Data, Datasets, & Replication

Benchmarks, artifacts, and reproducible pipelines.
